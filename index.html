She Stayed for the Kids

A classroom that never changed. Only the people did.

The Story

The room never settles. The schedule slips. Structure appears, then disappears just as quickly.

Teachers come in ready. Some leave. Some are removed. Some stay longer than they should.

Each one carries the same reason. She stayed for the kids.

“The room didn’t change. The people did.”

The Classroom

One assistant is worn down from carrying too much. One lead teacher struggles to hold a role without support. One new assistant walks in confident, then slowly understands.

Others were already here. Others have already left. Another is preparing to step into what remains.

And one sees clearly what works… but is never placed where it matters.
