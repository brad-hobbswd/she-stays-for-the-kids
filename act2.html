Act II

The Strain

What began as something manageable no longer holds its shape. The effort remains, though the results do not follow. The room continues, though not in a way that can be sustained without cost.

The gaps that were once subtle are now visible. What was overlooked can no longer be ignored. Communication does not connect. Structure does not remain. Responsibility shifts without direction, and the weight does not distribute evenly.

People begin to change.

Confidence gives way to questioning. Energy turns into endurance. What once felt like something that could be corrected begins to feel like something that must be survived.

Some reach their limit and step away. Others are removed when the strain becomes visible. Each change leaves the room less stable than before, even as it is filled again.

Those who remain carry more.

The pattern does not reset. It continues, building on what was never resolved.

Act II reveals what happens when effort meets a system that does not support it. It shows how people adapt, how they hold on, and how the same environment produces the same outcome, no matter who enters it.
